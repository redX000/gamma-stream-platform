# Promo Sequence — 3 Promotional Email Templates

> **Goal:** Convert warm subscribers into affiliate sales during launches, sales events, and limited-time offers.
> **Usage:** Customize and send as standalone broadcasts or triggered campaigns.
> **Best timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am subscriber local time.
> **Compliance note:** Always include clear disclosure per FTC guidelines (included in each template).

---

## Promo Email 1 — Flash Sale Announcement

> **Use case:** When a tool you promote runs a limited-time sale (common in Black Friday, end-of-quarter).

**Subject:** [TOOL_NAME] is [X]% off — ends [DATE]
**Preview text:** This is the cheapest it gets all year

---

Hey [First Name],

Quick heads up — [TOOL_NAME] just announced a [X]% discount that ends [DATE AT TIME ZONE].

I rarely send promotional emails. When I do, it's because the deal is genuinely worth it.

**The deal:**
- [PLAN_NAME] plan: ~~$[ORIGINAL_PRICE]/mo~~ → **$[SALE_PRICE]/mo**
- [PLAN_NAME_2] plan: ~~$[ORIGINAL_PRICE_2]/mo~~ → **$[SALE_PRICE_2]/mo**
- [Annual savings if applicable: e.g., "Annual billing saves an extra 20% on top of the sale"]

**Why [TOOL_NAME] specifically:**

[2–3 sentences on why you personally use or recommend this tool. Be specific — mention a result, a feature, or a workflow you rely on. This is what separates trusted recommendations from spam.]

I've been using it for [DURATION] and it's responsible for [SPECIFIC_RESULT — e.g., "roughly $300/month of my recurring affiliate income"].

→ **[Claim the deal before [DATE] →]([AFFILIATE_LINK])**

*Disclosure: This is an affiliate link. If you purchase through it, I earn a commission at no extra cost to you. I only promote tools I personally use and trust.*

[YOUR NAME]

P.S. If you're on the fence: this is the lowest price point I've seen for [TOOL_NAME] this year. Next time it comes up will probably be Black Friday. Your call.

## Promo Email 2 — New Tool Launch

> **Use case:** When you're introducing a new affiliate tool to your list for the first time — requires more trust-building upfront.

**Subject:** I've been testing [NEW_TOOL] for [X] weeks — here's my verdict
**Preview text:** [One honest sentence — good or bad — about the tool]

---

Hey [First Name],

For the past [X] weeks I've been testing [NEW_TOOL_NAME] — a new [CATEGORY: AI writing / SEO / automation] tool that's been getting a lot of buzz.

My verdict: **[CLEAR ONE-LINE VERDICT — e.g., "It's legitimately good for solo bloggers, but not worth it at scale."]**

**What I tested:**
- [TEST_1 — e.g., "Generated 5 full blog articles (1,500–2,000 words each)"]
- [TEST_2 — e.g., "Ran 10 SEO optimizations against existing articles"]
- [TEST_3 — e.g., "Compared output quality side-by-side with Jasper AI"]

**What I liked:**
- [PRO_1 — specific and honest]
- [PRO_2]
- [PRO_3]

**What I didn't like:**
- [CON_1 — always include at least one honest criticism]
- [CON_2]

**The bottom line:**

[TOOL_NAME] is worth trying if you [SPECIFIC_FIT_SCENARIO]. It's *not* the right tool if you [SPECIFIC_MISFIT_SCENARIO].

They're offering [FREE_TRIAL_DETAILS — e.g., "a 7-day free trial with no credit card required"], so there's no risk to testing it yourself.

→ **[Start your free [NEW_TOOL_NAME] trial →]([AFFILIATE_LINK])**

Full written review: [REVIEW_LINK]

*Disclosure: Affiliate link — I earn a commission if you purchase.*

[YOUR NAME]

## Promo Email 3 — Limited Bonus Offer

> **Use case:** Creating urgency around an affiliate offer by bundling it with your own exclusive bonus (guide, template, checklist, etc.).

**Subject:** [TOOL_NAME] + [BONUS_NAME] — only through [DATE]
**Preview text:** I'm adding [BONUS_DESCRIPTION] for anyone who signs up this week

---

Hey [First Name],

This week only, I'm doing something I don't usually do.

If you sign up for **[TOOL_NAME]** through my link before [DATE AT TIME], I'll personally send you **[BONUS_NAME]** — for free.

**What the bonus includes:**
- [BONUS_ITEM_1 — e.g., "My 90-day affiliate keyword research template (Google Sheets)"]
- [BONUS_ITEM_2 — e.g., "The exact Jasper AI prompt library I use for every article type"]
- [BONUS_ITEM_3 — e.g., "A recorded walkthrough of my full content pipeline setup"]
- **Value: $[BONUS_VALUE] — yours free with signup**

**Why [TOOL_NAME] right now:**

[TIMELY_REASON — e.g., "They just released their new SEO mode which closes the gap with Surfer SEO significantly. I've been testing it for two weeks and the output quality has genuinely improved."]

[SOCIAL_PROOF — e.g., "Three readers emailed me last week asking about this tool after seeing it mentioned in the Jasper vs. Copy.ai comparison — this is my full answer."]

**How to claim the bonus:**
1. Sign up via **[→ this link]([AFFILIATE_LINK])** (must use my link for me to track it)
2. Forward your purchase confirmation to [YOUR_EMAIL]
3. I'll send the bonus within 24 hours

This offer expires **[DATE] at [TIME] [TIMEZONE]**. I'm only doing this for this one campaign.

→ **[Sign up for [TOOL_NAME] + claim your bonus →]([AFFILIATE_LINK])**

*Disclosure: I earn a commission if you purchase through my link. The bonus is my own creation, provided by me — not the tool's company.*

Questions? Just reply to this email.

[YOUR NAME]
