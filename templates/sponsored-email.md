# Sponsored Email Template — FTC-Compliant Structure

Use this template for any newsletter issue that includes a paid sponsorship placement (Paved, direct brand deal, etc.).

FTC requirement: paid sponsorships must be **clearly disclosed** at the top of the email, before the sponsored content.

---

## Template

```
Subject: [Your normal subject line] + (Sponsored)
  — OR —
Subject: [Your normal subject line]
  (disclosure appears in the email body, not required in subject)
```

---

### Email Body

```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SPONSORED — Today's issue is brought to you by [Sponsor Name]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[SPONSOR CONTENT BLOCK — 100–200 words max]

Write this in your own voice. Describe:
  • What the product does
  • Who it's for
  • One specific benefit or use case
  • A clear CTA with affiliate/tracking link

Example:
  If you're spending hours writing content manually, [Tool Name] might be worth
  a look. It's an AI writing assistant built specifically for [use case], and
  unlike most tools I've tried, it actually [specific differentiator].

  → Try [Tool Name] free for 14 days: [affiliate link]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[Your regular newsletter content starts here]

Hi [first name],

...

---
You received this because you subscribed at gammacash.online.
Unsubscribe: [unsubscribe link]

This email contains a paid sponsorship from [Sponsor Name].
I only feature tools I believe are genuinely useful to this audience.
[Affiliate disclosure link: https://gammacash.online/affiliate-disclosure]
```

---

## Placement Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Position** | Sponsor block before main content — never buried |
| **Label** | Must say "Sponsored", "Advertisement", or "Paid partnership" |
| **Disclosure** | Footer must link to `/affiliate-disclosure` |
| **Honest voice** | Write in your own voice, not sponsor-provided copy |
| **One sponsor max** | One paid placement per email (ConvertKit best practice) |
| **Frequency** | Max 2 sponsored sends per week — stay reader-first |

## Sponsor Onboarding Checklist (via Paved)

When Paved matches you with a sponsor:

- [ ] Review sponsor's product — ensure it's relevant to AI/SaaS audience
- [ ] Accept or decline in Paved dashboard
- [ ] Get sponsor brief (copy, images, tracking link)
- [ ] Write your own version using the template above
- [ ] Schedule in ConvertKit
- [ ] After send: report open rate + click rate to Paved (auto-pulled in most cases)
- [ ] Invoice is generated automatically by Paved — paid to Payoneer

## Direct Deal Template (without Paved)

If a brand approaches you directly:

**Rate card (starting points):**
| List Size | Per Sponsored Send |
|-----------|-------------------|
| 1,000 subs | $30–$60 |
| 2,500 subs | $75–$150 |
| 5,000 subs | $150–$300 |

**Invoice to:** brand's billing email
**Payment method:** Payoneer invoice (send via Payoneer's "Request Payment" feature)
**Net terms:** Net-15 (payment within 15 days of send date)
