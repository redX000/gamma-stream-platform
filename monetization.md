## Revenue Stream 2: Display Advertising

### Tier 1 — Google AdSense (Month 3)
- Apply at: google.com/adsense
- Requirements: Original content, privacy policy, about page
- Expected RPM: $2–$8 per 1,000 sessions (AI/tech niche)
- At 10,000 sessions/month: $20–$80/month

### Tier 2 — Mediavine (Month 8–10)
- Requirements: 50,000 sessions/month
- Expected RPM: $15–$40 (4–6x AdSense)
- At 50,000 sessions: $750–$2,000/month

### Tier 3 — Raptive/AdThrive (Month 12+)
- Requirements: 100,000 pageviews/month
- Expected RPM: $20–$50
- At 100,000 pageviews: $2,000–$5,000/month

### Ad placement strategy
- Header banner (728×90)
- In-content ads (after 2nd paragraph)
- Sidebar (300×250)
- End of article (336×280)
- Never place ads that interrupt comparison tables

## Revenue Stream 3: Email Marketing

### List building strategy
- Lead magnet: "The Ultimate AI Tools Toolkit 2026" (free PDF)
- Pop-up trigger: Exit intent + 60 seconds on page
- Inline form: After every article
- Landing page: /free-toolkit

### Email monetization
- Affiliate promos: 2× per month (product launches, deals)
- Sponsored emails: $200–$500 per send at 3,000+ subscribers
- Digital product launches: 5-email launch sequences

### Email sequence structure
1. **Welcome (Day 0)**: Deliver lead magnet, set expectations
2. **Value (Day 2)**: Top 5 AI tools you need in 2026
3. **Case study (Day 4)**: How one freelancer saves 10hrs/week with AI
4. **Tool review (Day 6)**: Deep dive on one specific tool (affiliate link)
5. **Soft pitch (Day 8)**: "Here's what I use and recommend"

### Revenue from email
- 1,000 subscribers: $100–$500/month
- 3,000 subscribers: $500–$2,000/month
- 5,000 subscribers: $1,000–$4,000/month

## Revenue Stream 5: Brand Sponsorships

### When to start
- Begin outreach at Month 8
- Requirement: 2,000+ email subscribers OR 25,000+ monthly sessions

### Pricing guide
- Newsletter mention (1 sponsor): $150–$400/send
- Sponsored article: $300–$800 per post
- Sidebar banner (monthly): $100–$300
- Email sequence sponsor: $500–$1,500

### How to pitch sponsors
Target AI tool companies directly:
1. Find their affiliate/partnership page
2. Email their partnerships team
3. Share your traffic stats + audience demographics
4. Offer a trial sponsored post at discounted rate

### Target sponsors
- AI writing tools (Jasper, Copy.ai, Writesonic)
- SEO tools (Surfer, Ahrefs, Semrush)
- Productivity tools (Notion, ClickUp, Monday)
- Hosting/infrastructure (Cloudflare, Vercel, Digital Ocean)

## Revenue Stream 6: YouTube Shorts

### Channel strategy
- "Top 3 AI tools for [use case]" — weekly
- Quick tutorials: "How to do X with AI in 60 seconds"
- AI tool comparisons: "Jasper vs Copy.ai — 30 second verdict"

### Monetization
- YouTube Partner Program (1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours)
- Affiliate links in video descriptions
- Sponsorships once channel grows

### Revenue timeline
- 0–1,000 subs: $0 ad revenue, affiliate links only
- 1,000–10,000 subs: $50–$300/month ads + affiliates
- 10,000+ subs: $300–$2,000/month ads + affiliates + sponsorships
