# Gamma Stream — Content Strategy & SEO Keyword Plan

> **Objective:** Rank on page 1 of Google for buyer-intent AI tools keywords, drive organic traffic, and convert visitors to affiliate revenue through a systematic content mix of reviews (40%), comparisons (30%), tutorials (20%), and listicles (10%).

---

## 1. Niche & Audience

**Niche:** AI & SaaS tools for content creators, bloggers, and online entrepreneurs

**Primary audience:**
- Content creators publishing 3–10 articles/week who want to speed up production
- Bloggers monetizing through affiliate marketing who need reliable SEO tools
- Online entrepreneurs evaluating AI tools for business automation

**Search intent we target (in priority order):**
1. **Buyer intent** — "best X tool", "X review", "X vs Y" → highest conversion
2. **Commercial investigation** — "top X tools for Y", "X alternatives" → mid conversion
3. **Informational** — "how to do X with AI" → builds trust, captures email

---

## 2. Keyword Strategy

### Core Principles
- **Low competition first:** Target keywords with Keyword Difficulty (KD) < 30 in Months 1–3. Move to KD 30–50 in Months 4–9. Compete for KD 50+ after Month 9.
- **Long-tail over short-tail:** "best AI writing tool for real estate" ranks faster than "best AI writing tool" — and converts at 3× the rate.
- **Buyer-intent anchor:** Every article targets a keyword containing one of: "best", "review", "vs", "top", "how to", "alternative".

## 5. Content Type Specifications

### Reviews (40% of content)
- **Target word count:** 1,800–2,200 words
- **Structure:** Intro → What it is → Key features → Pricing → Pros/cons → Who it's for → Alternatives → Verdict + CTA
- **SEO targeting:** Keyword in H1, first paragraph, one H2, and meta description
- **CTA placement:** After pros/cons section AND in final verdict
- **Affiliate disclosure:** Above the fold (first 100 words)
- **Generator:** `node generator.js review "<keyword>" "<tool>"`

### Comparisons (30% of content)
- **Target word count:** 2,000–2,500 words
- **Structure:** TL;DR summary → Side-by-side table → What is X → What is Y → Feature comparison (6 categories) → Pricing → Which to choose → Verdict
- **SEO targeting:** Both tool names in H1, slug, and meta description
- **Conversion note:** Comparison articles convert at 2–3× the rate of reviews — prioritize after Month 2
- **Generator:** `node generator.js comparison "<keyword>" "<tool1>" "<tool2>"`

### Tutorials (20% of content)
- **Target word count:** 1,500–2,000 words
- **Structure:** What you'll learn → Prerequisites → Steps (numbered) → Worked example → Troubleshooting → Final thoughts + CTA
- **SEO targeting:** "How to" in H1, keyword in steps H2s
- **Conversion:** Lower direct conversion but highest email opt-in rate — always include a content upgrade
- **Generator:** `node generator.js tutorial "<keyword>" "<tool>"`

### Top-lists (10% of content)
- **Target word count:** 2,000–2,500 words
- **Structure:** Why trust us → Quick comparison table → Tool 1–7 (with mini-review each) → Buying guide → Final pick
- **SEO targeting:** Year + "best" + category in H1
- **Conversion:** High conversion for readers at the top of the funnel — include multiple affiliate links
- **Generator:** `node generator.js top-list "<keyword>" "<primary-tool>"`

## 6. On-Page SEO Checklist

Every article published must pass these checks (enforced by `seo-optimizer.js`):

- [ ] Target keyword in `<title>` tag (within first 60 characters)
- [ ] Target keyword in H1 (exact or close variant)
- [ ] Target keyword in first 100 words of body
- [ ] Target keyword in meta description (under 160 characters)
- [ ] Keyword density 0.5–2.5% (checked by `analyzeKeywordDensity()`)
- [ ] At least 2 H2 headings containing related keywords
- [ ] At least 1 comparison or data table
- [ ] Word count 1,500–2,500
- [ ] At least 1 internal link to a related article
- [ ] Affiliate links use `rel="sponsored nofollow"`
- [ ] Featured image with keyword in alt text
- [ ] Yoast SEO score ≥ 70 (set via publisher.js Yoast meta fields)
- [ ] SEO optimizer score ≥ 50 (auto-blocks publish below this threshold)

## 7. Internal Linking Strategy

**Goal:** Every article links to at least 2 other articles. This distributes PageRank, reduces bounce rate, and keeps readers on-site longer.

**Rules:**
- Reviews link to the relevant comparison (e.g., Jasper AI review → Jasper vs Copy.ai)
- Comparisons link to both individual reviews (e.g., Jasper vs Copy.ai → Jasper review + Copy.ai review)
- Tutorials link to the tool's review (e.g., How to use Jasper → Jasper AI review)
- Top-lists link to each individual tool's review
- Every article links to the lead magnet landing page with anchor text: "free AI tools guide"

**Pillar page structure (Month 6+):**

```
[PILLAR] Best AI Tools 2026 (top-list)
    ├── Jasper AI Review
    ├── Copy.ai Review
    ├── Writesonic Review
    ├── [PILLAR] Best AI Writing Tools (sub-pillar)
    │       ├── Jasper AI vs Copy.ai
    │       ├── Jasper AI vs Writesonic
    │       └── How to Use Jasper AI
    └── Surfer SEO Review
```

**Anchor text guidelines:**
- Use the target keyword of the destination article as anchor text where natural
- Avoid generic anchors: "click here", "read more", "this article"
- Limit to 1–2 internal links per 500 words to avoid over-optimization

## 10. Content Quality Standards

**Before publishing, every article must be:**
- Factually accurate — AI output must be human-verified for pricing, features, and claims
- Affiliate-disclosed — FTC disclosure above the fold and in the email footer
- Genuinely helpful — would a reader bookmark this if there were no affiliate links?
- Updated — pricing and features are checked against the tool's current website

**Red flags to reject before publishing:**
- Fabricated features or prices not verified against the official website
- Vague comparisons without specific data points
- No clear recommendation — wishy-washy "both are good" conclusions don't convert
- Keyword stuffing — density > 2.5% flagged by SEO optimizer and blocked from publish
